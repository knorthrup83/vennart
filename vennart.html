Mike Vennart's Musical Endeavors

Mike was a founding member of Manchester-based progressive rock band "Oceansize", which was active from 1998 until 2011. Since their breakup, Vennart has been a part of many other bands, the first of which he formed with former Oceansize partner Gambler. "British Theatre" are an electronica-alternative band. He and Gambler both later joined existing band "Biffy Clyro" as touring members, though they do not participate in writing the music. He also creates music under the moniker "Vennart", for which he is currently touring
